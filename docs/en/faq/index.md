# FAQ Overview

This section collects frequently asked questions and troubleshooting notes.

## Project Cost

All funding for this project came from the TH-MOS team budget. The total cost was roughly 400,000 RMB. The largest expense was motors, because we purchased motors from both Daran and ENCOS. The AGX is also expensive, at about 15,000 RMB per unit. In addition, each machining round cost about 20,000 RMB, and we did machining three times. The cost of 3D printing and PCB boards was relatively low, about 10,000 RMB in total.
